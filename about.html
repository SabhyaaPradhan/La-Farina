About La Farina

La Farina was founded in 2018. We're proud to be serving the local Christchurch and Sumner communities with our absolute commitment to quality.

The Dough

THE FIRST STEP IN GREAT PIZZA

Our dough is hand made every day, using the perfect blend of tradition and science. We combine different flours, fresh yeast, and just the right amount of hydration to make a signature dough which is light, flavoursome, and fragrant.

Then, the crucial ingredient: time. We let our dough sit for two days before using it, so it develops the complex flavours and airiness we're famous for. When it's time to use it, we hand stretch and spin each pizza base before applying our locally-sourced ingredients.
